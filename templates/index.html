How the Anemia Detection AI Works

  1. Conjunctiva Image Capture: A clear image of the lower eyelid conjunctiva is uploaded from the clinical environment.
  2. Preprocessing: The system normalizes color, resizes the image, and prepares it for the deep learning model.
  3. AI Inference: A trained convolutional neural network estimates anemia risk based on conjunctival pallor patterns.
  4. Risk Stratification: The model outputs a prediction, confidence score, and an approximate severity band (Normal, Mild, Moderate, Severe).
  5. Clinical Decision Support: The dashboard presents structured recommendations that should always be reviewed by a licensed clinician before action.

This tool is intended for screening and educational purposes only and must not replace professional medical diagnosis or laboratory testing.
